FORTNITE ON CHROMEBOOK
A bit harder than you think. First unblock your chromebook using the Unblock Chromebook button. Need help? Ask me IRL.

Method 1 - Nvidia (PC CONTROLS)

After, visit geforcenow and sign in (Make an account if you dont have one - do NOT use a school email account).
After this it may ask you to link accounts, or once you visit the home page there should be a button to.
Click this button or go to the link accounts page and link your Epic Games account Make one if you dont have one - you should if you play fortnite

Go to the home page, click fortnite, and play. The load times can be slow at times, but if you get down to like #70-#80 you will be on in no time.

Method 2 - XBOX

Better wait times? YES! Need a controller or phone? Also.. yes.

Visit xbox.com and sign in (Make an account if you dont have one - do NOT use a school email account).
After this select fortnite
Make sure you have a controller ready if you are on chromebook. Plug it in or bluetooth it before you hit play on fortnite.
Boom! Hit play and load in.
Once in game it gives you an option to link accounts. This isnt required.

Want to play on Phone (ios) even though its not on the store? Sure!
Go to xbox.com/play on Safari. Click share and add to home screen.
Then open the newly installed app and sign in. Click fortnite and play.
Boom! Touch controls.
